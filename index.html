A Day in the Life...

Morning

6:15 am Wake up

I feed my dog Margot and my cat Paisley as soon as I wake up. I heat water to make tea after feeding them. Then, I brush teeth, wash face, and get dressed for the day.

6:45 am Walk Dog

I take Margot for a 30 minute walk every morning. She has way too much energy and need to burn some before I leave for the day.

7:15 am Breakfast

After I get back I eat breakfast which is usually Greek yogurt with honey. I wash my dishes and prep my lunch for the day.

7:30 am Catch up on Work

I start my day by responding to emails and planning my day. Afterwards, I will work on projects like this one for Launchcode or job applications.

10:30 am Studio Visit

The Public Relations manager at Craft Alliance stops by to shoot photographs of me working in my home studio for the catalogue being made for the upcoming Artist-in-Residence Anniversary Exhibition.

11:00 am Lunch

I make a simple lunch before going to work, this week I have left-over quinoa chili.

11:15 am Bike to Work

I ride my bike to Craft Alliance to teach my summer camp. Itâ€™s better for me, my wallet and the environment.

Afternoon

12:00 pm Metals Camp

I get there early to prepare my materials and get everything set up. I am teaching 10-14 year olds how to make metal jewelry and sculptures.

3:45 pm Bike Home

The ride home is less fun, but I avoid a lot of traffic.

4:30 pm Pet Check

When I get home I need to feed my dog and cat and let Margo go outside for a while.

5:00 pm More Work

I continue working on projects and job applications. Soon some of this time can be spent making a sculpture for my next show in January.

Evening

6:30 pm Lecture at the Pulitzer

I have been meaning to see the exhibition at the Pulitzer since it went up. It will be over in a week so I go see a lecture on decolonization of women and the exhibition.

8:30 pm Walk Dog

Margot gets a longer evening walk which is good for both of us.

9:30 pm Dinner

I make a simple salad for dinner.

10:00 pm Shower

I prefer showering in the evening.

10:30 pm Work

I do more work or web surfing before going to bed.

11:30 pm Sleep

I read my science-fiction novel about coding before falling asleep.
